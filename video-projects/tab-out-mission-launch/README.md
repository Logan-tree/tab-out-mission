# Tab Out Mission Launch Video

## Purpose

Record a short demo video for Tab Out Mission and test whether people resonate with the pain point:

- browser tabs becoming messy during work
- task context getting lost
- quick thoughts disappearing into separate note apps
- turning open tabs into a focused Mission workspace

## Recording Approach

Record in short clips while speaking during operation. Each clip should cover one small idea or one feature. If a clip goes wrong, re-record only that clip and stitch them together later.

Target length: 4-6 minutes if keeping the full story, or under 3 minutes if cutting the Chrome Group comparison shorter.

## Core Narrative

1. I used to open too many tabs while working in the browser.
2. Zara Zhang's Tab Out has a clear product position: make it easier to close unnecessary tabs whenever you open a new tab.
3. Chrome's native tab groups help a little, but they still become another pile of saved contexts.
4. My own pain point is not only closing tabs, but switching between tasks while staying focused.
5. I adapted Zara's new-tab-page idea into Tab Out Mission.
6. The goal is not just managing tabs, but managing the current task context.
7. The public version should focus on Open Tabs, Mission Board, Saved Missions, Quick Notes, and simple completion history.
